Jessica Marie Ward - Me

Hi, I'm Jessica! Here you will find a few of my favorite things. If you'd like to know more about how I got to where I am today feel free to check out My Story page. I love playing pickleball. I enjoy getting the exercise and being able to compete in a sport as an adult. During the summer I love paddle boarding, hiking, and enjoying the midnight sun. I don't love cooking, but sure enjoy eating delicious foods. My favorite color is blue, sea turtles are my favorite animal, and I love the mountains.

My Family - Brax, Jay, Stockton

My family is my everything, and how could they not be! I am a proud momma to two little boys, with a third on the way. Being a boy mom is full of messes, owies, bugs, and dirt, but I wouldn't trade it for anything. What truly makes parenting special is having my best friend by my side through it all. Braxton is there through every up and down, and always keeps me smiling. I sure do love my little crew.

Alaska - My Home

The Last Frontier, Land of the Midnight Sun, The 49th State, all names of the beautiful place I get to call home. I love living in Alaska for so many reasons. It is a great place to raise kids, and beauty is around every corner. I love how the people here embrace every season regarless of temperature or weather. We go outside all year round! There are always adventures to be had, and the state is so big we will never run out of places to explore. Alaska is home.
