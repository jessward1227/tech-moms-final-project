Utah State University

My whole life I knew I wanted to be a teacher. In May of 2017 I finally accomplished that dream. I graduated with my Bachelors in Early Childhood Education. Before graduation I had already accepted a teaching position at Mountainville Academy in Alpine, Utah. It was my dream job teaching kindergarten at a fantastic charter school in a great community.

Teaching

My first year teaching was definitely full of ups and downs. Teaching in not an easy profession especially that first year. By my second year I already felt so much improvement. During that year I gave birth to my second son. I finished the school year, but decided to shift to being a preschool teacher in order to lessen my hours allowing me to be home more.

Transition to Tech

Teaching was my passion, but my kids will always be my everything. In order to be the mom I wanted to be I left teaching. I knew I wanted to continue working, but I needed a different career. IXN was my bridge into the tech industry. January of 2020 I started working as a data manager. This position grew over the next two years and confirmed tech is where I wanted to be.

What's Next

After a little over a year at IXN we were acquired by Insurance Technologies. I continued to learn and grow at this company for another year before leaving in March of 2022. At this time I became a full time mom due to a lack of childcare options. Over the last year I have expanded my knowledge, and plan on working towards becoming a front end software developer.
